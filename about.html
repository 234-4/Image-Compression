About Us

ImageCompressor is dedicated to providing a fast, free, and easy-to-use online image compression tool. Our mission is to help users reduce image file sizes for faster web performance, easier sharing, and better storage efficiency.

Our team is passionate about web performance and user privacy. We never store your images and all compression is done locally in your browser.
